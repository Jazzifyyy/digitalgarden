### Definition

We say that a [[6 - Main Notes/Statistic (inference fall)\|statistic]] $T(\mathbf{X})$ is **sufficient** for parameter $\theta$ if the conditional distribution of $\mathbf{X}$ given $T(\mathbf{X})=t$ is free of $\theta$.
