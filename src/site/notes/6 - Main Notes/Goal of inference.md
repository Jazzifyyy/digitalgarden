Suppose $X_{1},\dots,X_{n} \sim f_{\theta}$ are independent. We assume $\theta$ to be an *unknown parameter*. The goal is to do atleast one of the following:
1. To estimate $\theta$.
2. To test a hypothesis involving $\theta$.
