### Definition

A vector $\mathbf{v}$ of dimension $n$ is an **eigenvector** of a $n \times n$ matrix $A$, iff it satisfies
$$A\mathbf{v}= \lambda \mathbf{v}$$
for some scalar $\lambda$, referred to as the **eigenvalue** corresponding to $\mathbf{v}$.
