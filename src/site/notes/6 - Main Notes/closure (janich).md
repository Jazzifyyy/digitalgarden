### Definition

Suppose $(X,\mathcal{O})$ is a [[6 - Main Notes/topological space\|topological space]]. The set $\bar{B} \subseteq X$ is called the **closure** of $B$ iff for all $x \in \bar{B}$, $x$ is not an exterior point of $B$.
