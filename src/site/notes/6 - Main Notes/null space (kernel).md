### Definition

Suppose $T:U \rightarrow V$ is a [[6 - Main Notes/linear transformation\|linear transformation]]. The **null space** or **kernel** $N(T)$ associated with the transformation is defined as
$$N(T):= \{ x \in U \mid T(x)=\mathbf{0} \}.$$
