### Definition

Suppose $T:U \rightarrow V$ is a [[6 - Main Notes/linear transformation\|linear transformation]]. Then the **image** $\text{Im}(T)$ is defined as
$$\text{Im}(T):= \{ T(x) \mid x \in U \}.$$
