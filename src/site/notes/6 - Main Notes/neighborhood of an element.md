### Definition

Suppose $(X,\mathcal{O})$ is a [[6 - Main Notes/topological space\|topological space]]. We say that $U \subseteq X$ is a **neighborhood** of $x \in X$ iff there exists an open set $V \in \mathcal{O}$ such that $x \in V \subseteq U$.
