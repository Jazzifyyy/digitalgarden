### Statement

Suppose $X_{1},\dots,X_{n}$ is a random sample pdf $f_{X}$ and cdf $F_{X}$. 

Then the pdf of $X_{(j)}$ is given by
$$f_{X_{(j)}}(y) = \frac{n!}{(j-1)!(n-j)!}F_{X}(y)^{j-1}.[1-F_{X}(y)]^{n-j}.f_{X}(y).$$
