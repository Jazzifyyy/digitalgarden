### Definition

Suppose $U$ and $V$ are two vector spaces over $\mathbb{F}$. Then the map $T:U \rightarrow V$ is called a **linear transformation** iff for all $x,y \in U$ and $\alpha \in \mathbb{K}$, we have
$$T(x+y)= T(x)+ T(y)$$
and
$$T(\alpha x)= \alpha T(x).$$
