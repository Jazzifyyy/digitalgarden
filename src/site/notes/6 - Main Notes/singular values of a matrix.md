### Definition

The **singular values** $\sigma_{1},\dots, \sigma_{n}$ of a $m \times n$ matrix $A$ are the square roots $\sigma_{i}= \sqrt{ \lambda_{i} }>0$, of the nonzero eigenvalues of the associated Gram matrix $K=A^TA$.
