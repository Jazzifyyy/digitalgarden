### Definition

A real matrix $A$ is said to be **orthogonally diagonalisable** iff there exists a [[6 - Main Notes/diagonal matrix\|diagonal matrix]] $D$ and an [[6 - Main Notes/orthogonal matrix\|orthogonal matrix]] $S$ such that
$$D=S^TAS.$$
